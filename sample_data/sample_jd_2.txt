Machine Learning Engineer

About Us
We're building the next generation of AI-powered products. Our ML team works on cutting-edge problems in NLP, computer vision, and recommendation systems at scale.

Responsibilities
- Design, train, and deploy machine learning models for production systems
- Build and optimize data pipelines for model training and inference
- Develop NLP solutions for text classification, entity extraction, and semantic search
- Conduct experiments and A/B tests to validate model performance
- Collaborate with product and engineering teams to integrate ML into products
- Stay current with latest ML research and evaluate new techniques for adoption
- Write technical documentation and present findings to stakeholders

Required Qualifications
- MS or PhD in Computer Science, Machine Learning, Statistics, or related field
- 3+ years of experience building and deploying ML models in production
- Strong programming skills in Python
- Deep experience with ML frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch, or JAX)
- Experience with NLP techniques and transformer-based models
- Proficiency in SQL and data manipulation (Pandas, NumPy)
- Experience with cloud ML services (AWS SageMaker, GCP Vertex AI, or Azure ML)
- Strong foundation in statistics and experimental design

Nice to Have
- Experience with large language models (LLMs) and prompt engineering
- Knowledge of MLOps tools (MLflow, Kubeflow, Weights & Biases)
- Experience with distributed computing (Spark, Ray)
- Published research in ML/AI conferences (NeurIPS, ICML, ACL, etc.)
- Experience with recommendation systems or search ranking
- Familiarity with containerization and Kubernetes for model serving
